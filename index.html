Uber Drives - EDA

This project was a detailed analysis of Uber ride data to better understand how ride-sharing activity works. I studied different patterns such as total trips, completed and cancelled rides, most common drop-off locations, average fare, distance, ride duration, and time spent on trips. I also examined which days of the week had the highest average fares, which rides were the longest and shortest, which were the most and least expensive, and how much time usually passed before a trip began. To make the findings easier to understand, I used interactive visualizations to present the insights clearly.
